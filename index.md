## About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Yale University. My research focuses on optimization, machine learning theory, and mechanistic interpretation.

Before Yale, I received my B.S. in Data Science and Big Data Technology from the School of Data Science at Fudan University.

## Research Interests

- Optimization
- Machine learning theory
- Mechanistic interpretation
