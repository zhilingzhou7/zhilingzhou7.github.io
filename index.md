## About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Yale University. My research focuses on understanding how language models and transformer architectures learn, represent, and use structured information.

Before Yale, I received my B.S. in Data Science and Big Data Technology from the School of Data Science at Fudan University.

## Research Interests

- Mechanistic interpretability
- Language models
- Grammar learning
- Representation learning
- Understanding how transformers learn hierarchical structures
